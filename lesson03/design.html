Design

Color Scheme

The color scheme I chose for the Las Vegas Chamber of commerce is a combination of blue and green because blue represents stability, and green represents wealth. Business owners will feel this is a well-established organization and will help their business be stable and profitable.

Typography

The fonts I chose for the Las Vegas Chamber of commerce are 'Vollkorn' semibold weight 600 for the titles, and 'Manrope' light, with 300 weight for the rest of the text.
