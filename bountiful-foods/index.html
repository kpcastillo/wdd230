Southern California coast

In Southern California, the counties of San Diego, Orange, and Los Angeles are synonymous with the sun. It has incredible and stunning sandy beaches for just about any beach activity: Surfing, beach volleyball, camping, scuba diving, tide-pooling, people-watching, dog-walking, swimming, and just lying around sunbathing. You'll find comfy hotels, tasty restaurants, and many unique things to do near the beaches. The Southern California Coast is a beautiful place to live and an incredible vacation destination for those seeking sun and fun.
